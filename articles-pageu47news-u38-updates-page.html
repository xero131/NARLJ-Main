Article Title

Neural Mechanisms Underlying Cognitive Control: A Longitudinal Study

By Dr. Elizabeth Chen – Stanford University

Category: Neuroscience | Volume 12, Issue 2 (2025)

Published: June 1, 2025
Abstract

This longitudinal study explores how neural mechanisms support cognitive control over time. Using fMRI and behavioral data from a diverse participant group, the research identifies key changes in prefrontal cortex activity and suggests implications for developmental neuroscience and clinical intervention strategies.
